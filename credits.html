Research sources

Historical, cultural and natural-history content was compiled from multiple public sources, including Wikipedia (Kendhoo, Baa Atoll, Dhivehi beys), the Maldivian outlet The Edition (on the Magaamfulhu and Thabreyzgefaanu legend), Visit Maldives' experience pages on traditional medicine and dance, and several Baa Atoll dive and travel guides.
