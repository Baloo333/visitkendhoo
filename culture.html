Culture

A Living Island Heritage

Discover a community where the pulse of Maldivian life remains untouched. Kendhoo is a testament to the resilient, community-led preservation of traditions that define the authentic soul of the Maldives.

local_florist

Dhivehi Beys

Kendhoo is renowned nationwide for its deep-rooted traditional herbal healing practices. Generations of knowledge are passed down, utilizing island flora to treat ailments. People travel from afar seeking the wisdom of Kendhoo's healers.

Learn about healing herbs arrow_forward

The Beat of the Island

When the sun sets, the rhythm of Kendhoo comes alive. Eid and local festivals are marked with the thunderous beats of Boduberu drumming. It's a visceral, high-energy communal celebration where chanting, dancing, and rhythm unite the entire island in joyous fervor.
