🤿 Excursions & Activities

From Sandbanks to Manta Trains

Discover the vibrant marine life of the Baa Atoll UNESCO Biosphere Reserve, just a short boat ride from Kendhoo's shores.

Signature Experiences

waves Jul - Oct Season

Hanifaru Bay Encounters

A short boat ride to the world's largest known manta ray feeding aggregation. Snorkel alongside these gentle giants in a protected marine area.

Private Sandbank Escape

Quiet isolation on the pristine white sands of the Fasdūtherē chain.

Sunset Fishing

Traditional dhoni experience ending with a beach barbecue.
