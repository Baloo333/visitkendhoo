Getting There

Kendhoo is located in Baa Atoll, a UNESCO Biosphere Reserve. Getting here is an adventure in itself, offering breathtaking views of the archipelago.

flight

Domestic Flight

A 20-minute scenic flight from Velana International Airport (Male) to Dharavandhoo Domestic Airport, followed by a 40-minute speedboat ride to Kendhoo.

sailing

Direct Speedboat

Direct speedboat transfers from Male operate on scheduled days. The journey takes approximately 2.5 to 3 hours, offering a direct route across the atolls.

directions_boat

Local Ferry

For the slow traveler. The public ferry from Male takes longer (often requiring an overnight stay in Baa Atoll capital) but offers the most authentic local experience.

Stay with Locals

Experience genuine Maldivian hospitality. Kendhoo offers a selection of welcoming guesthouses run by local families.

Unlike isolated resort islands, staying in a guesthouse immerses you in the daily rhythm of island life. Wake up to the sound of the ocean, enjoy home-cooked Maldivian cuisine, and learn about the local culture directly from the residents.

  • check_circle Modern amenities with local charm
  • check_circle Direct access to pristine beaches
  • check_circle Support the local island economy

Local Etiquette

Kendhoo is an inhabited local island. Respecting the local Muslim culture ensures a harmonious and enriching experience for everyone.
